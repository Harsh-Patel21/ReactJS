useState – state ko manage karne ke liye.

useEffect – side effects handle karne ke liye
(jaise API call, DOM manipulation, event listener).

useContext – global state ko consume karne ke liye without prop drilling.

useReducer – complex state management ke liye
(Redux jaisa chhota version).

useRef – mutable values hold karne ke liye jo re-render trigger na karein,
ya DOM access karne ke liye.

useMemo & useCallback – optimization ke liye, unnecessary re-renders avoid karne ke liye.
